## What Solidify is  
Solidify is a comprehensive web application and admin portal designed to provide users with tools and resources for managing sustainability-related tasks effectively.

## Getting Started  
1. Visit the [deployed app](https://solidify-fe.vercel.app) to explore features and functionalities.  
2. Create an account or log in if you already have one.

## Authentication  
- **Login:** Enter your credentials to access your account.  
- **Forgot Password:** Click on the 'Forgot Password?' link to reset your password.  
- **Reset Password:** Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.
- **Change Password:** Go to 'Settings' > 'Profile' to change your password.
- **Logout:** Click on the 'Logout' button in the navigation menu.

## Navigation Overview  
The application consists of several sections accessible through the navigation bar at the top:
- Dashboard
- Carbon Entry
- News
- Sustainability Choices
- Library
- Chat
- Find SME (Subject Matter Expert)
- Settings & Profile

## Dashboard  
The dashboard provides insights into your sustainability efforts and displays key metrics at a glance.

## Carbon Entry  
Users can log carbon entries to track their carbon footprint. Follow the prompts to enter data.

## News  
Stay updated with the latest news in sustainability by visiting this section regularly.

## Sustainability Choices  
Explore various options for making sustainable choices in your daily life.

## Library  
Access a collection of resources and documents related to sustainability and best practices.

## Chat  
Interact with other users and experts via the chat feature for assistance and discussions.

## Find SME  
Search for subject matter experts to get personalized advice and guidance.

## Settings & Profile  
Manage your account settings, update your profile information, and customize your experience.

## Admin Access Rules  
Administrators have special access rights, which include:  
- Managing users  
- Monitoring application usage  
- Accessing sensitive data  

## Admin Routes  
- `/admin/dashboard`: Admin dashboard  
- `/admin/users`: User management  
- `/admin/reports`: Generate reports

## Troubleshooting  
If you encounter any issues, consult the FAQ section or contact support.
